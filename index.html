Gabarecycle

We are here to provide an environmentally friendly,efficient and effective garbage management system.
The current garbage collection system includes routine garbage trucks doing rounds daily or weekly,which not only doesn't cover every zone of the residential areas but is also completely inefficient

Gabarecycle is a cost effective web based application for Stay Clean NGO to utilize available resources to efficiently manage the overwhelming amounts of garbage collected each day. As well emptying latrines and septic tanks in residential areas.
